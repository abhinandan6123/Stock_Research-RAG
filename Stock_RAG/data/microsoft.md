# Microsoft (MSFT)

Microsoft is the largest enterprise AI platform company through its Azure cloud and OpenAI partnership.

## AI Strategy
- $13B+ investment in OpenAI; exclusive cloud partner for GPT-4/GPT-4o
- Copilot integrated across Office 365, Teams, GitHub, Dynamics 365
- Azure OpenAI Service: enterprise API access to GPT models

## Capex Commitment
- $80B AI infrastructure capex planned for FY2025
- Building new data centers across US, Europe, Asia
- Power procurement: nuclear (Three Mile Island restart), renewables

## Revenue Impact
- Azure AI revenue growing at 7% incremental contribution per quarter
- GitHub Copilot: 1.8M paid subscribers (2024), $19/month
- Microsoft 365 Copilot: $30/user/month enterprise add-on

## Competitive Position
- Azure: #2 cloud behind AWS, gaining share in AI workloads
- OpenAI partnership creates differentiation vs Google Cloud, AWS
- Enterprise sales force advantage: existing Office/Azure relationships

## Risks
- OpenAI relationship dependency; Anthropic deal with Google/AWS threatens exclusivity
- Copilot ROI questioned by enterprises; churn risk if productivity gains unclear
- Regulatory scrutiny: EU AI Act, FTC antitrust interest in OpenAI deal

## Valuation
- Forward P/E: ~32x
- Revenue Growth: ~17% YoY
- Gross Margin: ~70%
